About Bwinga Wildlife Safaris

Bwinga Wildlife Safaris Limited is a premier tour operator based in Kampala, Uganda, specializing in unforgettable gorilla trekking experiences in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.

Founded and led by CEO Ahumuza Justus Joe, our company is dedicated to providing exceptional wildlife encounters while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and conservation efforts.

Based in Nkwenda-Buhoma, Kanungu district, we have unparalleled expertise in navigating the Bwindi forest and ensuring our guests have safe, memorable encounters with Uganda's famous mountain gorillas.

Beyond gorilla trekking, we offer comprehensive safari packages that showcase Uganda's diverse wildlife, landscapes, and cultures across the entire country.
